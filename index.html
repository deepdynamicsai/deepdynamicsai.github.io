Research

DeepDynamics Lab investigates how structure in dynamics can guide the design of learning systems. We combine dynamical systems, information geometry, and modern ML to build models that are stable, sample-efficient, and scientifically grounded.

  • Reservoir computing & Echo State Networks (ESNs): designing reservoirs with geometry/topology, stability guarantees, and robust prediction.
  • Operator learning (Koopman / neural operators): lifting nonlinear dynamics to linearizable representations for forecasting and control.
  • Intrinsic dimension & complexity of state representations: measuring and leveraging low-dimensional structure in high-dimensional recurrent states.
  • Rigorous evaluation on chaotic systems: Lorenz, Rössler, and related benchmarks; uncertainty and long-horizon behavior.
